02 / Foreword

A LETTER FROM THE CEO

“The barrier to youth-led change has never been talent. It has never been ambition. It has never been readiness. It has been access.”

Around the world, young people are already solving the problems their communities face — water scarcity, mental health, climate resilience, economic exclusion. What they lack isn't vision. It's capital, trust, and the permission to be taken seriously.

RIVET exists to close that gap. We fund the first yes. We protect youth ownership of their work. We stand between the institutions that hold resources and the young leaders who hold the answers — and we make sure the bridge actually carries weight.

Dr. Eric D. Dawson · CEO & Founder

04 / The Capital Pathway

THREE TIERS.
ONE PIPELINE.

Most funders force young innovators to look like institutions before they'll write the first check. RIVET flips it: meet them where they are, then grow the capital as they grow.

Tier 01

ACCESS

$250 – $999

Small, fast, flexible. Turns an idea into a first implementation — often the first money a young leader has ever been trusted with.

Tier 02

BOOST

$1,000 – $9,999

Grow, deepen, and stabilize. Moves a proven pilot into a repeatable program with staff, tools, and measurement in place.

Tier 03

SCALE

$10,000+

Expand and institutionalize. Backs the transition from movement to organization — with the systems, partnerships, and follow-on capital to sustain it.

THE TRUST COMPOUNDING CYCLE

Every small grant we deploy unlocks the next one — not just for the recipient, but for the ecosystem around them. When a young leader's first yes works, the institutions around them start paying attention.

The fastest way to accelerate youth-led change isn't a bigger first check. It's a faster second one.
